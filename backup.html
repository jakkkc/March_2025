March 2025 Marketing Report

Introduction

March 2025 has been a pivotal month for the digital marketing efforts of HPC and HPT. Key milestones included the official launch of HPC Facebook page and HPT Instagram page, both of which have shown steady growth. Management approved and facilitated investments in essential software and equipment to enhance digital marketing efficiency. This report evaluates social media performance, financial allocations, challenges, and strategic recommendations, along with achievable 90-day growth targets starting 1st April, 2025 to ensure continued digital expansion.

HPC Key highlights from Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, TikTok, and Google Business


1. Facebook: HPC Facebook page was launched on February 28, 2025, and has grown steadily, reaching 250 followers by the end of March. Engagement per post averages 10-15 likes, 5-10 comments, and 3-5 shares.
2. Instagram: The Instagram page continues to build momentum, with 300 new followers and reels generating positive reactions.
3. YouTube: The channel has two videos with 150 and 200 views respectively, with an average watch time of 2 minutes.
4. LinkedIn: HPC's LinkedIn page has 50 followers, with minimal engagement.
5. TikTok: TikTok is an emerging opportunity for HPC, with 100 new followers and an average of 200-300 views per post.
6. Google Business: HPC Google Business page has gained traction with 20 new reviews in March, averaging a 4.7-star rating.

HPT Key highlights from Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, TikTok, and Google Business


1. Facebook: HPT Facebook page continues to maintain a solid audience base, growing to 400 followers.
2. Instagram: Since its launch on February 28, 2025, the HPT Instagram page has gained 220 followers.
3. YouTube: HPT YouTube presence is still in its early stages, with two uploaded videos accumulating 120 and 180 views.
4. LinkedIn: With 40 followers, HPT LinkedIn presents an opportunity to strengthen its professional image.
5. TikTok: HPT TikTok presence has been minimal, with 5 short-form videos receiving an average of 150-250 views each.
6. Google Business: HPT Google Business profile has been updated and received 15 new reviews this month.

Financial Allocation & Budget

The budget initially set aside for boosting social media posts was reallocated to settle an outstanding Instagram debt. Despite budget constraints, the team successfully maintained and grew organic reach across platforms. Management has approved the acquisition of essential software and equipment, which will enhance digital marketing operations and content production efficiency.

Challenges & Recommendations

Challenges: Limited engagement due to the absence of paid promotions. Budget constraints impacted advertising. Slow audience growth on new platforms. No professional video content production, which is crucial for higher engagement.

Recommendations: Investing in professional video production, influencer partnerships, and targeted advertising. Engagement-driven content such as live Q&A sessions, polls, and giveaways should be implemented.

90-Day Growth Targets

  • Targets include increasing HPC Facebook followers to 600.

  • Instagram to 700, and YouTube to 200 subscribers.

  • HPT aims to grow Facebook to 800 followers.

  • Instagram to 500, and TikTok to 400 followers.
